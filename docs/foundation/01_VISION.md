# Engineering Governance Runtime Vision

## Vision

Establish continuously trustworthy software engineering by integrating Software Industrialisation, DevSecOps, Continuous Security, Continuous Compliance, and Executable Engineering Governance into a coherent, technology-agnostic architecture.

## Mission

Provide a reusable reference implementation demonstrating how governance can become an operational engineering capability rather than remain primarily a document-centric review activity.

## Core Problem

Modern software-intensive systems change continuously. Requirements, architecture, source code, dependencies, pipelines, infrastructure, releases, and operational configurations evolve at different speeds and across multiple organisations and suppliers.

Traditional governance remains necessary, but periodic reviews and manually reconstructed evidence cannot provide sufficient confidence at the speed of modern engineering.

## Strategic Objectives

1. Industrialise software engineering through repeatable and measurable capabilities.
2. Integrate Security into the full lifecycle through DevSecOps.
3. Enable continuous generation and evaluation of engineering and security evidence.
4. Make governance executable where objective verification is possible.
5. Preserve human authority for risk-based and non-deterministic decisions.
6. Maintain traceability from governance intent to controls, evidence, evaluations, exceptions, and results.
7. Remain independent of individual tools and vendors.
8. Support multiple engineering platforms through adapters.
9. Publish versioned governance baselines.
10. Provide human-readable and machine-readable outcomes.

## Primary Architectural Objective

The primary objective is **trust in engineering decisions**.

## Scope Boundary

The current milestone focuses on Software Industrialisation, DevSecOps, Security, Continuous Compliance, Executable Engineering Governance, and Architecture Governance where it supports runtime-verifiable engineering decisions.

The AI Factory is intentionally excluded from the current milestone.
